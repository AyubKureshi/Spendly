Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information Spendly collects, how we use it, and what choices you have. We keep things simple: Spendly is a personal expense tracker, and we only collect what we need to make that work for you.

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect the information you provide directly — typically your name, email address, and a password. When you use the service, we store the expense records you choose to enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you add.

We also collect basic technical information such as your browser type, device, and IP address, which we use to keep the service secure and diagnose problems. We do not collect your financial account credentials and we never ask for your bank or card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to operate Spendly — to display your expenses, generate summaries and category breakdowns, remember your preferences, and keep your account secure. We do not use your expense data to build advertising profiles, and we do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may use aggregated, non-identifying statistics (for example, total number of users) to understand how Spendly is used and to guide future improvements. These statistics cannot be used to identify you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers operated by trusted infrastructure providers. We take reasonable steps to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, including encryption in transit and access controls for staff.

You can export or delete your expense records at any time from your account settings. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data from our active systems within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small number of third party services to operate — for example, hosting and email delivery. These providers receive only the information needed to perform their function and are contractually obligated to protect your data.

We do not integrate advertising networks, analytics tools that track you across other sites, or social media trackers. If we ever add a new third party service that affects your data, we will update this policy first.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or if you would like to access, correct, or delete the data we hold about you, please reach out to us through the channels listed on the Spendly website. We will respond as quickly as we can.
